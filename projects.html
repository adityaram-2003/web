The Modules Connectivity Diagram

The Pre-Processing Module employs Keras for data augmentation and normalization of X-ray images.

The Fracture Detection Module utilizes ResNet50 to analyze these prepared images and accurately identify fractures.

The Web Integration Module employs Flask to create a user-friendly interface for seamless image upload and processing.

Lastly, the Model Evaluation Module assesses the detection model's performance using key metrics such as accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score, facilitating ongoing improvements and optimizations.

The Architecture

Our system begins by uploading an image from our dataset, followed by TensorFlow preprocessing to convert it to the desired resolution. The ResNet50 algorithm then analyzes the image across 50 layers to detect fractures. The output is subsequently displayed on an HTML page via Flask, ensuring accurate fracture detection and user-friendly interaction through the web interface.

The Dataset

Downloaded from Kaggle, the dataset comprises a variety of bone X-ray images categorized into three distinct bone types.

Elbow

The training dataset contains over 1800 bone images specifically focused on the elbow samples. These images are classified based on diverse bone structures and positions, ensuring comprehensive coverage of elbow fractures.

Hand

The Hand category includes over 2100 bone images, representing diverse configurations and orientations of hand bones for visual assessment of fracture patterns.

Shoulder

The Shoulder class comprises over 2750 bone images in the training dataset, featuring detailed shoulder bone anatomy and orientations essential for training models to distinguish between various types of shoulder fractures.

Our model evaluation indicates an accuracy of 87%, precision of 83%, recall of 67%, and F1 score of 74%.

Summary
This technology aims to enhance the accuracy and precision of diagnosing bone fractures through a comprehensive approach. It extracts intricate features from medical images using the Convolutional Neural Network ResNet50 algorithm, and it is versatile enough to handle various imaging sources.
